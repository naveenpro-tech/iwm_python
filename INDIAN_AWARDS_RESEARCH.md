## 1. NATIONAL FILM AWARDS (Government of India)

**Organization:** Directorate of Film Festivals, Ministry of Information and Broadcasting  
**Country:** India  
**Language:** All Indian languages  
**Prestige Level:** Highest (National)  
**Established:** 1954

### Categories:
1. **Feature Films:**
   - Best Feature Film (Golden Lotus Award)
   - Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment
   - Best Film on National Integration
   - Best Film on Social Issues
   - Best Environmental Film
   - Best Film on Family Welfare
   - Nargis Dutt Award for Best Feature Film on National Integration
   - Indira Gandhi Award for Best Debut Film of a Director
   - Best Children's Film

2. **Direction & Writing:**
   - Best Director
   - Best Screenplay (Original)
   - Best Screenplay (Adapted)
   - Best Dialogues
   - Best Story

3. **Acting:**
   - Best Actor (Silver Lotus Award)
   - Best Actress (Silver Lotus Award)
   - Best Supporting Actor
   - Best Supporting Actress
   - Best Child Artist

4. **Technical Awards:**
   - Best Cinematography
   - Best Editing
   - Best Production Design
   - Best Costume Design
   - Best Make-up Artist
   - Best Audiography (Location Sound Recordist)
   - Best Audiography (Sound Designer)
   - Best Audiography (Re-recordist of the Final Mixed Track)
   - Best Special Effects
   - Best Visual Effects
   - Best Stunt Choreographer

5. **Music:**
   - Best Music Direction (Songs)
   - Best Music Direction (Background Score)
   - Best Male Playback Singer
   - Best Female Playback Singer
   - Best Lyrics

6. **Language-Specific:**
   - Best Hindi Film
   - Best Tamil Film
   - Best Telugu Film
   - Best Malayalam Film
   - Best Kannada Film
   - Best Bengali Film
   - Best Marathi Film
   - Best Gujarati Film
   - Best Punjabi Film
   - Best Assamese Film
   - Best Odia Film
   - And other regional languages
